Daily Mental Wellness Tips

Making small daily changes can greatly improve your mental health. Here are some ideas to help you get started:

Examples of Self-Care Routines

  • Getting enough sleep

    • Good sleep directly supports better attention, school performance, emotional regulation, and impulse control.

  • Eating healthy and staying hydrated

    • A healthy diet can boost brain function and improve mood.

  • Focus on positivity

    • A positive attitude can help us cope with difficult situations and challenges more effectively.

  • Setting goals and priorities

    • Provides tangible evidence of growth and improvement which helps reinforce a positive mindset.

  • Exercise Regularly

    • Helps with better brain function, mood enhancment, and boost self-esteem.

Top 5 Things to avoid

  1. Overloading your schedule

    2. Causes burnout and increases stress and anxiety

  2. Neglecting self-care

    3. Causes emotional exhaustion and physical health decrease.

  3. Social media overload

    4. Disrupts sleep and causes social isolation, and a reduction in attention span.

  4. Ignoring professional or any help

    5. Not getting any help can cause your mental health to get worse which could lead to substance abuse or self harm.

  5. Substance abuse

    6. On top of a physical health decline, it causes an increase in depression and anciety. Also causes social and relationship problems.
